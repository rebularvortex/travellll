Nepali Cuisine

Dal Bhat Tarkari

NPR 250-350

The national dish of Nepal! A wholesome plate of lentil curry (dal), steamed rice (bhat), and an assortment of vegetable curries (tarkari). Often accompanied by pickles and papad.

Must Try With:
Achar (pickle) • Papad • Ghee

Momo

NPR 200-300

Steamed or fried dumplings filled with buffalo meat, chicken, or vegetables. Served with spicy tomato chutney. A favorite street food across Nepal!

Types:
Steamed • Fried • Jhol Momo (in soup)

Thukpa

NPR 280-400

Hearty Tibetan-influenced noodle soup with vegetables, meat, and fragrant spices. Perfect for cold Himalayan evenings.

Best In:
Winter • Mountain regions

Sekuwa

NPR 350-500

Grilled skewers of marinated meat (chicken, mutton, or buffalo). A popular street food and festival delicacy with smoky flavors.

Perfect Pairing:
Chutney • Roti

Lassi

NPR 80-150

Creamy yogurt-based drink, sweet or salty. The mango lassi is especially popular during summer months.

Flavors:
Sweet • Salty • Mango

Sel Roti

NPR 50-100

Traditional rice flour doughnut, deep-fried to golden perfection. A festival favorite during Tihar and Dashain.

Best During:
Festivals • Tea time
