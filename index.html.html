Tourism and travel in Nepal is a major part of the country’s identity and economy. Known for its stunning Himalayan mountains, including Mount Everest, Nepal attracts visitors from all over the world for trekking, adventure sports, and cultural experiences. The country also offers rich traditions, ancient temples, and diverse landscapes ranging from snowy peaks to green valleys. Whether exploring Kathmandu’s heritage sites or hiking through peaceful mountain trails, Nepal provides a unique and memorable travel experience.
